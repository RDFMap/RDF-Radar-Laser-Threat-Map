Want a more accurate route than OSRM?
OSRM is solid for geometry, but it’s OpenStreetMap-based and not traffic-aware. If you want something closer to the route Google would actually send you on right now, use Google Maps + MapstoGPX and import the GPX here.
1) Click Open Google Maps above.
2) In Google Maps, make sure the route looks right (tolls / avoid highways / whatever).
3) Copy the Google Maps directions URL from your browser/mobile app.
4) Open mapstogpx.com, paste the URL, and download the GPX.
5) Click Import GPX here and select the GPX file. This will draw that route and run the same route analysis.
